Welcome to The Spice Garden

Authentic Indian cuisine crafted with love, tradition, and the finest spices from across the subcontinent. Serving Mumbai since 2010.

Why Dine With Us?

Fresh Ingredients

We source the freshest vegetables, spices, and herbs from local farms every single morning.

Family Recipes

Our recipes have been passed down through four generations -authentic flavors you won't find elsewhere .

Cozy Ambience

Warm lighting, traditional decor, and soft music create the perfect setting for an unforgettable meal.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Chef Raj Patel, The Spice Garden started as a small family kitchen serving homestyle Indian food. Today, we're proud to be one of the city's most beloved restaurants, serving over 500 happy customers every week.

We believe food is more than fuel — it's a way to bring people together, celebrate culture, and create memories that last a lifetime.

“Cooking is an art, but eating is a joy.” — Chef Raj Patel
